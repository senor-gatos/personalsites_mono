The basement is officially open for business.

This is where I'll be dumping thoughts, half-baked ideas, project logs, and the occasional unhinged observation about the universe. No algorithm, no engagement metrics, no hot takes optimized for clicks — just a nerd with too many hobbies and a website to fill.

## What to expect

- **Hobby logs** — progress updates on whatever I'm obsessively learning this month
- **Project write-ups** — how things got built, what broke, what I learned
- **Articles** — longer-form thoughts on sci-fi, technology, space, weird history, or whatever rabbit hole I fell down
- **Shenanigans** — miscellaneous

## Why a workshop?

Because every nerd needs a workbench, and mine happens to be digital. The real basement workspace is significantly more cluttered and smells like solder.

Welcome. Mind the cables.
